Now the real fun begins. 

We know it’s tempting to dive right into FullStory and start watching user sessions. But while you’re exploring, let us suggest a few ways to start turning your new data into actionable insights. 

Search

Click the Search Everyone button to get started, then choose any combination of User Scope and Event Scope queries to build a search as broad or as narrow as you’d like.

Get acquainted with search

Share

Click the share button on any user session to leave a comment and pin the moment to your team’s highlight stream. It’s a bookmark, stickynote, and sharing feature all rolled into one.

Collaborate

Your left-hand navigation bar holds two powerful tools for teams: the Highlights tab shows a timeline of everyone’s segments, and Team Segments contains all the search segments saved by your team.
