Planning + Billing Redesign

One of my first projects at ShootProof was to redesign the Planning and Billing Page to better reflect the updated visualson the rest of the web app.

The original Planning and Billing Page was cluttered and visually overwhelming, making it difficult to identify a current plan and change to another one. The new design cut down on stark colors and utilized iconography to confirm features within each plan.

Wireframe + Flows Redesign

The old wireframes and annotations used at ShootProof did not properly relay information to the ShootProof design and users during testing. The visuals of the wireframes were conflicting and had too much contrast. My goal was to soften the visual design of the wireframes and adapt them to high fidelity visuals of the ShootProof app.

The new wireframes I created were done in an Axure RP8 library, giving the UX design team extremely quick and concise method of mocking up the web app in full size and mobile screens. Everything from buttons to forms to basic page components were accounted for in the Axure library.

I also made fullsize and mobile wireframe flows and annotations for the wireframe components. The new annotations were much softer on the eyes visually so that the wireframes could be the forefront of the design at hand.
