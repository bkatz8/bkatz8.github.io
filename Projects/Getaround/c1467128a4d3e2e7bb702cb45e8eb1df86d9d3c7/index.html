Getaround Design Challenge

A redesigned user and vehicle rating system to instill trust in the Getaround community

Role: UX/UI Design | Timeframe: 52 hours

Tools Used: Sketch, Flinto, Invision, HandSketches, HTML+CSS+JS

View Project

Introducing Vehicle and User Rating from Getaround

Getaround is a new rating system for its mobile application, giving renters and owners more confidence and trust than ever before. Getaround's certified vehicles give renters peace of mind that they're the best rides around. Getaround renters with pro status let renters know that their vehciles are in goof hands. You can even see renter certifications and vehicle mechanic checkups to make sure you're making the best decision possible.

Problem

"We allow renters to rate trips they’ve taken and owners to state whether they would trust the renter to rent their car again. We currently use this data internally so our support team can address issues and bad user behavior. As we become a community regulated marketplace, we want to surface user and car ratings in the product in instill confidence and trust in our users."

These user groups were the following- owners, users/riders, and Getaround. The first step of this process was renting a Getaround car and interviewing the owner. Revised Design Objective

With all of my research, initial ideation, and user personas in hand, I decided to create a revised design objective. The rationale behind the narrowing of the design prompt was to ensure that the design accurately addressed the needs of all three user groups.

"Design an experience that surfaces car and user ratings via an updated search and rental process in order to instill confidence and trust in the Getaround community."

Proposed Final Solution Mockups + Flows

Below are the final high-level wireframes and flows for the renter and owner flows. The renter flow is to surface owner and vehicle ratings while the owner flow surfaces renter ratings to determine rental decisions.

Searching and Filtering to Surface Ratings

To surface car, owner, and renter ratings in the Getaround mobile app I redesigned search and filtering functionalty. Filters now can display only "verified" vehicles. These are vehicles that have over 20 trips and consistently receive 5 star ratings inside and out with minimal mehcanical issues. Another option that was explored in wireframes and flows was the concept of using CarFax reports and mechanic inspections to further verify cars.

What's Getaround Verified?

Getaround verified vehicles receive a green check mark inside of search to surface their owners commitment to exellence in the mobile application. In the new filter functionality, the call to action button also displays the number of results that the filter has yielded.

Surfacing Car and User Ratings

Apart from surfacing vehicle ratings while scrolling through a filtered search, users can now view the rating breakdown of how particular vehicles. This brings to light any mechanical, interior, or exterior issues that the vehicle may have had.

User + Rider Ratings

To surface user ratings and instill confidence in owners to trust their vehicles with others, renters can become "Getaround Pro" users and receive a pink star on their profile picture.

Surfacing Vehicle Ratings Before Trips

Below are the high-fidelity mockups and flows for rating a vehicle before a trip. I chose a breadcrumb flow to reduce the user's cognitive load during ratings and to shorten the perceived time of vehicle review.

Reporting Vehicle Issues During Trips

In addition to contacting owners and Getaround about vehicle issues during a trip, it would instill more confidence and trust in the community to use these issues as a criteria for vehicle ratings.

Once a user has encountered and opted to report a vehicle issue during their trip, they're prompted with a review flow with the same aesthetics and interactions as a vehicle rating before a trip.

Approving Users and Surfacing Ratings

To instill trust and confidence in vehicle owners, I've redesigned the approval process in the Getaround mobile experience. When an owner receives a request from a renter for their vehicle, they can review that indivual's Getaround profile. There the renter's Getaround trips, ratings, and trustworthiness is displayed. If they have earned a Getaround pro status, this will be displayed as well to convey addtional trust in the renter.

Why's the profile picture blurred out?

To avoid discrimination amongst the Getaround community, their profile picture will be hidden from owners until they have approved or denied that renter's request. The renter's profile and ratings will give the owner plenty of data to make a decision.
