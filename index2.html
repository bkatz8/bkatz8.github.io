What I Do

Research

Taking a design from its first sketch on a napkin to a polished masterpiece on the shelf takes time, but seeing a project through to the end is what I do.

Ideation

Even after a product sees production, the design process keeps on churning. All designs can be bettered; I constantly strive to improve my design through practice and more practice.

Development

Whether it's going out to interview users or digging through textbooks for hours, I think that pre-informed design is imperative for a successful project.

Iteration

After specifying what I want in a design, putting my pen to paper and fingers to keyboard gets the creative juices flowing.

Hey, I'm Ben...

I’m a current industrial design student at Georgia Tech. I’m a passionate believer that the best design comes from our experiences in the world around us, not in a classroom. I think road trips, mountain biking, and homemade sushi are something else. Soul food and funk music are my kryptonite. Take a look through my work and if you think something’s cool, make sure to get in touch.
