About Me

I’m a 4th year industrial design student at Georgia Tech and I blend the digital and physical worlds together into delightful experiences. I’m a passionate believer that the best design comes from the experiences in the world around us, not in a classroom. I think road trips, pick-up soccer, and homemade sushi are something else. Soul food and funk music are my kryptonite. Take a look through my work and if you think something’s cool, make sure to get in touch.
