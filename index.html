What I Do

Insight

I take time to understand users and their needs, environment, etc. to make informed design decisions, period. All good design is founded in a deep understanding of the "who, why, and how."

Design

Whether it's putting pen to paper for a round of sketching or going out to intervew users, design my way of creating experiences that make people smile everyday.

Reflection

Design is a constantly changing task. Looking back to learn from where things went great and where they could have gone better is a critical part of everything I do.

Hey, I'm Ben.

I’m a 4th year industrial design student at Georgia Tech and I blend the digital and physical worlds together into delightful experiences. I’m a passionate believer that the best design comes from the experiences in the world around us, not in a classroom. I think road trips, pick-up soccer, and homemade sushi are something else. Soul food and funk music are my kryptonite. Take a look through my work and if you think something’s cool, make sure to get in touch.
