Hello, my name is Ben Katz. I’m a Product Designer, and where we’re going we don’t need roads.

What I Do

User Experience

Whether I'm deep into wireframe land or going out into the world for user testing, my UX practice is all about those magical little moments in a purposeful design that makes someone smile.

Interaction

I regularly use my studies in traditional industrial design to create digital and physical interactions that are a delight to engage with and bring out the complex emotions every product should incite.

Research

All thought out design stems from knowing not only the "who's, what's, and where's," but more importantly the "why's." I love taking deep looks into people's lives to really get to the bottom of what makes them tick.
