What I Do

Research + Insight
Design with a Purpose

Design is much more than powerful user experience and elegant interfaces. It's the aggregation of processes that are needed to create comething with a purpose. All my design work begins with collecting qualitative and quantitaive data. Then taking a step back and analyzing the situation efficiently informs great design opportunities.

Concept + Design
Digital & Physical Worlds

Once a design direction is established, it's time to put pen to paper. Whether it's digital sketching, wireframing, or an old-fashioned ballpoint pen, informed rapid concept generation leads to beautiful things. I'm fascinated by the connection between digital interactions and the physical and emotional reponses an elegant design can elicit.

Reflection + Repition
Bringing Design Full Circle

Even after a final design is determined, the design process is never done. It's a constantly turining wheel that's more exciting with every turn. Conscious reflection on what worked and what didn't after a project is conlcuded is only a step in the right direction. I'm a passionate believer that all designs can be improved, and so can I as a designer.

Hey, I'm Ben...

I’m a 3rd year industrial design student at Georgia Tech and I blend the digital and physical worlds together into delightful experiences.
